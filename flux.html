Flux 5

iOS 11.0 - 13.3.x untethered jailbreak

To use Flux, you must install it to your device using Cydia Impactor or another sideloading method (like Xcode).

Impactor is a tool by Saurik (the guy who made Cydia) that is used to install apps from outside the App Store. It is fully safe.

To install Flux, you first download the .ipa file using the link below. Next, you open Impactor on your computer, and plug in your device. Drag and drop the file you downloaded over your device's name. You will be asked for your Apple ID email and password. Put them in. This is safe (as far as anyone knows) and only sent to Apple. Once this is done, the app will install.

If you choose to use another sideloading method (or Impactor is down), the instructions may be different. If you are currently jailbroken with another jailbreak, you can install it using Filza and AppSync
