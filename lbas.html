What is LBAS?

LBAS stands for Low Budget Animation Studios. It is a sub-company run by Kiayeorg. We animate the KIAYE Episodes on Vine, and are building an iOS game that we would like to release in the near future.

Is there anything you guys have made that we can check out?

Yes! Right now, you can check out our Kiaye Episodes, and you can follow us on Twitter @Sn0wCh1ld for more information on our upcoming iOS app. Technically, this is also ours, but it's a game that was never completed, and never will be. It was also created when we weren't called LBAS, and Kiayeorg wasn't even around yet.

What's with your name?

Well, we are extremely low budget (the only thing paid for was the Mac used for development and we will purchase a subscription to the iOS developer program), and we animate stuff, like cartoons and the upcoming game.

I checked Sn0wCh1ld's Twitter. He's been working on an app for over a year and it's not out yet?

Not exactly. You see, the first app was called "Gnop" and never came out, because the way it was built wasn't very good, and when iOS 8 came out, it broke. It was completely unfixable, so he started on the new app - the right way. That app has been in development for a couple months, but school slowed it down. It's ready now, but it needs some more content, and money for the iOS developer program subscription.

How broke are you guys?

Very.
