I bet you're wondering what is a "KIAYE!". Now, that is a very good question. The answer is: "because". That is the only reason you will ever need. It's pretty much just an experiment to see if one can find our site, just by being hit with its name so many times.

Before you ask, no, KIAYE! doesn't have a meaning. It's just a word that was used as some kind of a joke at one point, and I wanted to own a domain with it. Someone bought the .com minutes before me, but released it after I bought the .org. When the .org expires, if kiaye.com is available, I will buy it instead.
