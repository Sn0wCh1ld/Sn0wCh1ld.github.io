How to bypass OurPact (probably)

Introduction

OurPact is a tool that allows parents to take near-absolute control over their children's devices. I've personally never been subject to it, but I've met people who have had it installed on their phones, and I really felt bad for them. I've come to despise the program, and I want to help anyone controlled by it to have a way our. Here are two guides to beating OurPact, each with increasing difficulty, but also with increasing effectiveness. The guides are only for iOS devices.

The first guide should remove OurPact your iOS device without notifying the parent. Effective against most parents

The second guide should remove OurPact your iOS device without notifying the parent. It will also add a new profile that looks like the official OurPact profile. If a parent checked the Settings app, they would not be easily able to tell the profile had been tampered with. Effective against most curious/relatively tech savvy/relatively untrusting parents


Easier Guide

Step 1

Enable Airplane mode on your device.

Step 2

In the Settings app, navigate to General > Device Management.

Step 3

Select "Device System Profile", with the name "OurPact" underneath it.

Step 4

Press "Remove Management" and put in your password. When prompted, press remove.

Step 5

Once the profile is removed, press the home button and power button until you see an Apple logo.

Step 6

When the device is booted back up, unlock it, turn off Airplane mode, and hope it worked.
